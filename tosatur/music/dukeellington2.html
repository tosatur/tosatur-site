Duke Ellington Version 2

The second version of the original aidscore track. I made this version because the app I was using to publish music to Spotify didn't allow tracks under 2:30 in length.

It also had a bunch of other weird restrictions, like preventing you from putting any text on your album cover other than the name of the album or your artist name. I stopped using that app and publishing my music to Spotify soon after. This song also sucks complete ass.

The album cover is a vectorised and cropped screenshot of the movie Romeo and Juliet (1968), directed by Franco Zeffirelli.

Released 2018-09-20.
