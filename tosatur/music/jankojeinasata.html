JAN KOJE INASATA

An ambient track inspired by the work of Jake Chudnow.

The album cover is an edited illustration of the Norse goddess Hel, drawn by Johannes Gehrts in 1889. I didn't know this when I made the cover, to me it was just a cool looking image I found on Wikimedia random files.

The title of this album is in Toki Pona.

Released 2022-05-16.
