JOSEPHINE FALLS

I visited Josephine Falls in 2020. It is a small waterfall in Wooroonooran National Park in Queensland, Australia, about 75km south of Cairns. It is a popular swimming spot, albeit extremely cold. It was very foggy when I took these photos, but I think it made them look a lot nicer. I also saw a cool bird.
