B3: BOUNCING BOYS BEAUTIFUL

B3: Bouncing Boys Beautiful is an idle game about dropping men out of a tube into a big chamber and watching them bounce. You get points every time they collide with the floor or walls.

You can get upgrades to get more points per bounce, make the men more bouncy, and more.

There are philosophical themes in this game. I will not elaborate.
