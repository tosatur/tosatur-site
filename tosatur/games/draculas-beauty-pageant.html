Oh No! The Draculas Beauty Pageant Is In One Minute And I Need To Raise My Draculas!!!

Oh No! The Draculas Beauty Pageant Is In One Minute And I Need To Raise My Draculas!!! is a game I made with Bonicle for Mehu Jam 12. The game was made in 48 hours. I was responsible for most of the programming.

In the game, you have 60 seconds to feed and grow your draculas, little Sea Monkey-like creatures, before the beauty pageant begins.

This was my first game made in Godot. The game can be played in the browser.
