WOODBOY DOES A JUMP

Woodboy Does a Jump is a 2D platformer I made. You must reach the end of the level before the giant laser catches up to you.

This wasn't my first foray into game development; I had made games before, but this is the earliest one I still have saved and can be reasonably called a "game."

Despite this, it isnt a good game by any measure, although there are some parts I am proud of, like the level select area, which is a platforming level in itself.
