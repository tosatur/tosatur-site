Numeral pronouns and correlatives

Numbers in Afaarmiša can be used like correlatives, that is, either added to a word as a prefix, or on their own.

Ordinal numbers and frequencies

Ordinal numbers in Afaarmiša are formed by adding the suffix -(i)ka to the end of the number. Similarly, frequency numbers (once, twice, etc.) can be formed by adding the suffix -(i)cola to the end of a number. These words can be used as indefinite pronouns, adjectival verbs, or adverbs (when conjugated as if an adjectival verb),
