Afaarmiša

by tosatur

Afaarmiša is an agglutinative language, loosely based on Finnish, Italian, and Japanese. I started this conlang around the beginning of 2021, and after a long hiatus, I am returning to this conlang as of 2022. I plan to flesh out the vocabulary, add more translations, create a writing system, and redo some of the grammar that may not have made sense the first time.

If you have any questions about the language, or more likely I've just made some really dumb mistake somewhere, you can contact me.

A changelog for Afaarmiša can be seen here.
